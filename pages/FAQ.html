How do I return purchased item?

Once the need for return arise, items can be sent back via courier or drop-off at any of our retail outlet for refund. T & C apply


How many days do I have to return an item?

From the day of delivery, you have 29 days to return the item for refund. T & C apply


Can I return personalised item?

All personalised items are non-refundable


How do I return purchased item?

To return purchased items, make sure it is within 29 days after delivery, receipts are available and the product is not damaged. The intended item(s) should be sent to our retail outlet by courier or drop-off. T & C apply


Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, our delivery destinations include Europe, Austraila, Africa, and United states.


How do I track my order?

Your orders can not be tracked. However, all orders within Europe takes 4-7 business days while outside Europe takes 7-10 working days.


Do you offer discount?

Yes, discounts are offered on special occassions. Discount availability is always displayed on our home page. Keep an eye.


How can I contact support?

You can get assistance by clicking the "Contact us" link.


How long does an order take?

All orders within Europe takes 4-7 business days while outside Europe takes 7-10 working days.


How can I make a order?

To make an order, click the desired item and proceed with payment. Successful payment means Order is Successful.


Do you accept Bitcoin?

We do note accept bitcoin or any other cryptocurrency as payment


Can I cancell my order after making payment?

Yes, all order can be cancelled on or before delivery. However, it might take longer to get a refund. Expect refund within 20 working days
